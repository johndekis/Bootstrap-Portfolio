Expenster:

This app allows users to login with a google account and manage their expenses form their dashboard. Users can add, delete, or update their expenses and then sort them by date or amount. The user may also filter by certain dates, using the date range picker, or by specific text using the search box. Expenster is made with react and redux and uses firebase to store data. Jest is used for testing.
