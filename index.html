About

After high school, I studied nutrition for a year before persuing an Associate's degree in Culinary Arts. I spent about 7 years working in restaurants and kitchens and in that time I learned a great deal. I honed my knife skills and practiced plating technique in some of the best restaurants in the country, but after working my way up the ranks, I found the career outlook and lifestyle factors that came along with professional cooking to be pretty weak.

After some time spent just working jobs to get by, I began to think about a new career for myself. It seemed logical to me that I should find something to capitalize on my aptitiude for art and creativity, but this time with a greater emphasis on a more satisfying career. Then a friend of mine got me interested in coding and programming and after looking into it for myself, everything seemed to click into place.

So now I study programming language all the time. I am all about learning new techniques and practicing their application. I would love to be a full stack developer working on sites and apps and then look towards video game creation. I also love video games. Maybe I can make the next Skyrim. When I am not working on school or playing video games, I am usually in the gym practicing my Kickboxing or eating the delicious food that I still know how to make
